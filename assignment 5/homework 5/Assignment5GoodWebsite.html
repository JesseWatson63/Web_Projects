Good Websites And The Creation process

The Planning Processe

Are you paying attention? This is the most important part, PLANNING. Planning a website will always and should always be the first step. Where a picture sits, where a link is, where a advertisement pops out is essential to the success or failure to a website. Experienced web developers know that a better product comes from a plan. Failure to plan is a failed website.

Color, Pictures and a touch of simplicity

According to Nick La Web design can be deceptively difficult, as it involves achieving a design that is both usable and pleasing, delivers information and builds brand, is technically sound and visually coherent. This certainly holds true if one wishes to have a successful website or a career in web design. Color strongly influence the feeling someone gets when viewing your website. If you want a good uplifting feeling use soft a color, if you want a deep emotional feeling like in a video game use dark color. Images are another strong influence. Whether you are trying to advertise your business or ad a link that you hope users will go to, an image will certainly influence them in many ways. Adding simplicity to the navigation in your page is essential to a successful website. Have you ever been on a website looking for something specific and just found it impossible to find what you need. Quickly one closes the web page and searches for a new giving them the information that they want. This article that Mr. La wrote is a very good one on key features of what makes a website stand out among the others. He has a clear message as to what a web site should have and how it should be laid out.
